The Studio

Quietly precise. Unmistakably yours.

Amara Lash is a private, appointment-only studio in Brooklyn dedicated to the craft of custom eyelash extensions. Every set is designed in collaboration with you — your eye shape, your lifestyle, the look you want to live in.

With over five years of specialized training and certification in classic, hybrid, volume, and mega volume techniques, we work lash-by-lash with premium-grade materials that protect the integrity of your natural lashes.

Step inside and exhale. The studio is intentionally calm, low-light, and unhurried — an hour or two that belongs entirely to you.

Frequently Asked

Good to know.

Arrive with clean, makeup-free eyes — no mascara, eyeliner, or heavy moisturizer. Skip caffeine if you tend to flutter, and please remove contact lenses before your appointment. Plan for up to 2.5 hours so we’re never rushed.
Lash extensions follow your natural growth cycle and typically last 3–4 weeks. For best retention and a seamless look, we recommend booking a fill every 2–3 weeks. After three weeks, a full set is usually needed.
Avoid water, steam, and oils for the first 24 hours. After that, cleanse daily with a lash-safe foam, brush gently every morning with a clean spoolie, and skip oil-based makeup removers. Sleep on your back or side when possible — never face down.
We carry sensitive-skin adhesives and offer patch testing 48 hours in advance for first-time clients with a history of allergies. Please share any sensitivities or recent eye procedures when you book so we can plan accordingly.
Out of respect for the studio and other clients, we ask for at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule. Same-day cancellations and no-shows are charged 50% of the service. We deeply appreciate your understanding.
